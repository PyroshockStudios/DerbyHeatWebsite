Since we are making an ambitious game, we need as motivated artists as we have programmers and designers! Currently, the sole artist is me (Tristan, a.k.a. ZilverBlade), and because of this, we have had a tough time trying to keep artwork up with the development, causing constant slowdowns.

If you are willing to work with us (while we cannot provide payment yet, we will guarantee a percentage of revenue in the future) Please contact us at contact@pyroshockstudios.com!

The game has quite envolved art targets, ranging from environment to customisation, but currently the most critical art is the following:

  • Weapons (e.g. Firearms)
  • Cars (ranging from small cars to very large, preferrably deformable)
  • Props (both destructible and non-destructible)
  • Buildings
  • Vegetation

There are also other artistic aspects that we would love to work on, but are much lower priority:

  • Car liveries/skins (partially being worked on by MakoTheUndead)
  • Stickers/badges/decals
  • Car interiors (low fidelity, as we will not have an interior camera mode)
  • UI/UX (we use RmlUi so if you know HTML/CSS this is a very approachable task!)

As a result of having to balance many different tasks within Pyroshock Studios, we feasibly cannot put as much care into the assets (neither in quality nor quantity) as we would like.
